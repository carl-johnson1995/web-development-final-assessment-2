The Story

the story of gta san andreas is about cj who returns to los santos after the death of his mother and he tries to fix his life and help his family and his gang the story shows many place in the game like los santos san fierro and las venturas and each place has mission and character that help cj or fight him the game world is big and full of action and the player can explore street car and building the story also talks about trust family and betrayal and how cj must choose the right path to save grove street
